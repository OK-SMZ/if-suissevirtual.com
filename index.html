Welcome to Suisse Virtual

About Us

SUISSE Virtual is a Virtual Airline, inspired by SWISS, and has the goal of bringing unparalleled excellence to the Infinite Flight Community, whilst still maintaining the incredibly exciting and joyous aspects of a Virtual Airline Membership.

Everything we do is done with the utmost quality; a perfect representation of the excellence shown by the real-life Swiss Air Lines, and of Swiss design in general.

Our team is made up of dedicated staff who bring substantial experience in VA development and operations; thus allowing our pilots to reach new heights and experience a real-world airline through our immersive Crew Centre and interact with fellow pilots and staff through our Discord channel. We use real-life routes for Swiss and Edelweiss which contribute to our diverse route network.

Zurich

Zurich Internation Airport

Zurich Airport (ZÜRICh Flughafen - LSZH) is one of Europe's major aviation hubs and a must-visit destination in Infinite Flight. Located in Switzerland, the airport can accommodate a wide range of aircraft, regional jes to long-haul airlines. Its realistic layout Infinite Flight virtual pilots to experience authentic operations, with accurately placed parking stands and taxiways following real-world procedures.

Whether you're flying a long-haul route to Asia or the Americas or a short European hop hop with Swiss International Airline, Zurich is a versatile airport suited for all types of flights. With ATC services frequently available durin live events. You can enjoy an immersive and professional experience.

Geneva

Geneva International Airport

Geneva Airport (Genève Aéroport - LSGG) is a key international gateway in Switzerland and exciting destination in Infinite Flight. Located near the French border, its offer stunning views of the Alps and Lake Geneva during the approach and departure. The airport features a single but long and busy runwa, capable of handling both short-haul regional flights and long-haul intercontinental operations. In Infinite Fligh, Geneva is modeled with realistic taxiways, terminals and parking positions, providing and authentic experience for virtual pilots.

Whether you're flying a European route with EasyJet Switzeland or an international jouney with Swiss International Airlines, Geneva offers a dynamic and diverse fying experience.
